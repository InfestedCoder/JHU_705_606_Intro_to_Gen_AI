2. Reasoning Agent response

A fully preferred overlap does not exist because Alpha prefers 9 AM–12 PM, Beta prefers 1 PM–4 PM, and Gamma prefers 11 AM–1 PM. The best shared day is Thursday, since none of the agents are unavailable then.

The best compromise time is Thursday at 12 PM. This fits Alpha’s morning preference at the edge, fits Gamma’s late-morning preference exactly, and is close to Beta’s afternoon preference.
